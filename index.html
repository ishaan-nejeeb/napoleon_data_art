Napoleon's Russian Campaign of 1812-1813

This interactive visualization attempts to show Charles Minard's original map of Napoleon's Russian Campaign in the form of an interactive narrative. The number of soldiers left in main army are represented my green circles (color of Napoleon's army uniforms) rushing about the scene. Each circle represents 1000 soldier. The background also changes depending on whether the Grand Army is attacking ( ) or retreating ( ). Press the right arrow key to go a step forwrd in time and the left arrow key to go backward in time. Each step is also accompanied by a descriptive text.
